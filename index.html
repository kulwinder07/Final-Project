Click me to Know How to Play?
  • To start the game, Click the "Roll Dice" Button
  • To advance to the next round,Click the "Roll Dice" Button again
  • After three rounds the game is over and the final score is calculated
  • If you want to play again,Click the "New Game" button or refresh the page


Click to view rules of the game:
  • Each round,You and computer will roll a pair of dice.
  • Your score for that round will be based on sum of the dice
  • If any of the two dice comes up as 1 then the score of that round is 0.
  • If both the dice shows same number then score of the round is total of two dice times two.
